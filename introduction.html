About Me!

Long story short, I survived.

Either Sophia or Soffi by my closest friends, I describe myself as two sides of one coin. I can be spontaneous, cheerful and a bit manic when I drink too much coffee. At times, I am grumpy, emotionally overwhelmed and fake bravado is one of my favorite facades. Yet, I can't deny that I strive to be the best, always.

Personalities that I can describe myself in keywords:

  • Resourceful.
  • Curious.
  • Bold.
  • Wilful when I can be.

I am the second child in my family, easily overlooked as well as needed when my family members can't find a mutual decision. My own parents divorced when I was young, (thanks for the trauma, Mama and Daddy!) yet I am glad that I have been raised by my mother rather than my father. My sister, beauty with brains are the family's expectations as well as the one who carries the burden the most besides my mother. My brother, annoying yet he is useful when I need him the most can be such a boy sometimes. Nevertheless, they are my family and I will not trade it with anyone else on this Earth.

But poetry, beauty, romance, love - these are what we stay alive for.

I do consider myself as privileged. I have been given the opportunity to learn English at a very young age and it has become an advantage as well as a risk to other people. My efficiency in English, a foreign language strikes deep inside of their hearts; calling me out as I am nothing but 'colonized.' In reality, however, it is imperative and a rizq I always take for granted, never realizing that I have been luckier than other children who have to rely on hard work and plenty of efforts just to be able to speak in this colonizer's tongue. Yet, I will never forget my true roots; a Malaysian, a Malay girl and a Muslimah.
